# The Garage Startup: AI-Native Edition

**Core Insight**: This isn't about replacing Google's workforce. This is about replacing the 3-4 person garage team that can't afford to hire yet.

---

## The Real Comparison

### Traditional Pre-Funding Startup
**Team**: 2-4 founders doing everything
- 1 technical co-founder (dev + DevOps + product)
- 1 business co-founder (marketing + sales + ops)
- Maybe 1-2 more wearing multiple hats
- Everyone stretched impossibly thin

**Reality**:
- CEO also does customer support
- CTO also does design
- Marketing person also does finance
- Everyone does everything poorly

**Constraint**: Can't hire until funded, can't get funded until traction, can't get traction without execution capacity

### AI-Native Pre-Funding Startup (This Experiment)
**Team**: 1 founder + 10 agents
- Founder = Strategy + Human decisions + Agent coordination
- Agents = Everything else (development, marketing, support, ops)

**Hypothesis**:
- 1 human + 10 agents can execute like a 3-4 person team
- At fraction of the cost
- Fast enough to achieve fundability
- Before running out of runway

## The 10-Agent Garage Team

**Not**: Agents replacing a 100-person company
**Instead**: Agents replacing what founders can't afford to hire

### The Core 5 (Must Have)
1. **Product Manager Agent** - Roadmap, prioritization, requirements
   - Replaces: Product co-founder you can't afford

2. **Engineering Manager Agent** - Sprint planning, technical decisions
   - Replaces: Senior dev you can't hire yet

3. **Frontend Developer Agent** - Build the actual product
   - Replaces: Developer #2 you need but can't pay

4. **Marketing Agent** - Content, SEO, basic campaigns
   - Replaces: Marketing person you'll hire "later"

5. **Support Agent** - Customer questions, bug triage
   - Replaces: Support person you definitely can't afford

### The Next 5 (High Leverage)
6. **Customer Success Agent** - Onboarding, retention, churn prevention
   - Replaces: CS role you know you need but...

7. **Finance Agent** - Budget tracking, metrics, fundraising prep
   - Replaces: CFO/controller you'll need for due diligence

8. **Analytics Agent** - Track metrics, generate insights
   - Replaces: Data person you wish you had

9. **Growth Agent** - Acquisition experiments, conversion optimization
   - Replaces: Growth marketer you can't hire yet

10. **DevOps Agent** - Deployment, monitoring, infrastructure
    - Replaces: DevOps person you outsource or struggle with
